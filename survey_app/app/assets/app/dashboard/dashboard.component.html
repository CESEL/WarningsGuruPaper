Warnings Study

We are presently doing a study on the introduction of static analysis warnings (Findbugs & Jlint) in the commits of projects and their usefulness to software developers. In the process of this analysis we observed the new warnings on commits and are trying to assess if they are useful to the developer who worked on the commit

More information can be obtain by contacting Louis-Philippe Querel (l_querel@encs.concordia.ca)
